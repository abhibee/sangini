Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is an Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains, above the Urubamba River valley. It was Built in the 15th century and was later abandoned.It is renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone walls that is huge blocks joined without the use of molten rocks, intriguing buildings that play on astronomical placements and panoramic views. Its exact use remains a mystery.It's Elevation is 2,430 m. It was Abandoned in the year 1539. It was founded in the 1450 BCE.
