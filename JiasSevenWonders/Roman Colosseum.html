Roman Colosseum



The Colosseum also known as the Flavian Amphitheatre, is an amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome, Italy. Built of travertine limestone, tuff, and brick-faced concrete, it used tobe the largest amphitheatre ever built at that time and held 50,000 viewers. It's Construction started in 70 AD.It's Height is 48 m.It is a Vespasian architecture.
