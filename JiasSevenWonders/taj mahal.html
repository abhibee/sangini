Taj Mahal



The Taj Mahal is an white marble mausoleum on the bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra. It was approved in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to show how much he loved his favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal; it houses the tomb of Mumtaz Mahal, and even shahjahan himself.The construction started in the year 1632 .It's height is 73meters.It's architects were Ustad Ahmad Lahour and Ustad Isa
